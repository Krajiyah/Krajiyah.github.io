About Myself

I am a passionate developer who enjoys learning new things, whether its on the job or in my spare time. I try to explore every end of the stack from frontend (mobile + web) to backend (RESTful apis, socket servers, distributed locking, queueing, logging, and metering), and sometimes low level firmware on occasion (IoT + BLE). I always consider security at each layer (CSRFs, XSS Sanitization, signed JWTs, SDK security scans, api credentials, key vaults, and HSMs). I never forget the importance of testing, and continuing to ensure product delivery is a success (TDD, Jenkins, CircleCI, Calabash, and Docker). I have found that this adaptibility allows me to easily come up to speed on new teams.
